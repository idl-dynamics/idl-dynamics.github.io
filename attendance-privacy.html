Privacy Policy for IDL Attendance

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

IDL Attendance collects minimal data necessary to function as a classroom management tool. This includes basic account information (email, name) provided during registration via Supabase.

2. Location Data (GPS)

Our "Location-Based Attendance" feature requires temporary access to your device's location (GPS). This data is ONLY used momentarily to verify your presence within the classroom radius defined by the instructor. We do not track your location in the background, nor do we store your location history.

3. Messaging and Phone Numbers

Our app facilitates secure communication between teachers and students without requiring or sharing personal phone numbers. Messages are securely stored and strictly limited to classroom context.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. Your information is never sold to third parties.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at: idldynamics@gmail.com
